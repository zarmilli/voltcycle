Our Policy

01

All service bookings require a confirmed appointment. Walk-ins are welcome but subject to availability and may incur longer wait times.

02

A diagnostic fee applies to all electrical assessments. This fee is waived if you proceed with the recommended repair.

03

Uncollected bikes left beyond 14 days after service completion may incur a storage fee. Please collect your bike promptly.

04

We warranty all labour for 30 days and parts for 90 days from the date of service. Warranty is void if the bike is tampered with by a third party.
